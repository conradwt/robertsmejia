    About Me

    Hello, my name is Robert S. Mejia.  I am a 3D Artist/Game Developer living in Downey, California. My goal is to obtain a position where I can utilize my traditional and technical art skills in assisting in the production of quality work at a quality studio.  You can view or download my RESUME.
     
    EXPERIENCE
     
    Technical Skills
    • Maya, 3ds Max, Mudbox, ZBrush, Photoshop, Illustrator, 3D-Coat, xNormal, DDO, Mari, Topogun, UVLayout, Unity 3D, Final Cut Studio, Premiere,  After Effects, AutoCAD, Revit, FormZ, Javascript, CSharp 
     
    Qualifications
    • Strong traditional fine art skills - illustration, painting, composition, human anatomy/organic forms, color theory, perspective, 2D & 3D design
    • Video game product development - high and low poly modeling, organic, hard surface, prop and asset modeling, texture painting, material setup, character skinning, mapping techniques, UV layout, animation, character rigging, model from concepts and a single photograph, UI/UX design
    • Good communication, organizational, time management, and interpersonal skills
